About

I remember my birthday when I was around seventeen years old, it was a misty saturday morning outside my parent's house which was surrounded by cornfields.
Seventeen didn't really matter that much to me or birthdays in general but I still loved and love saturday morning cartoons.

To my surprise the bell rang, it must've been nine in the morning and even worse the person in front of the door was there to see me! Turns out it was one of my best friends with a bag of candy and she was visiting with the express intention of eating candy in bed and watching spirited away. Which I probably only had seen once at the time, so that's what we did and it's still one of the sweetest memories I have of my friend.

That's mostly likely when the idea for this restaurant was born, four establishments later and we're still going strong on food and anime!

PS: the name comes from the soy-sauce brand my mom always uses.
